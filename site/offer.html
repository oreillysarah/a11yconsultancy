What do you get with this Offer?

Accessibility is about making sure websites can be used by people of all abilities, including elderly and disabled people. In the UK and other countries, access to digital services is a legal requirement, just like access to physical services like shops, restaurants, and transport. How accessible is your website to people with disabilities? Does it conform to WCAG 2.0 accessibility guidelines? Full accessibility audits are a significant investment but my basic accessibility review will give you an idea of your current standard of accessibility and level of conformance to WCAG 2.0 (international) guidelines. I'll test a sample of pages for the most common and important accessibility problems, and then provide a short report with my recommendations so that you know how to prioritise your next steps.

Please note: This high-level accessibility review can only give an indication as to the general level of accessibility. It is not a full accessibility audit and positive results do not guarantee that your website meets all of the accessibility guidelines or legal requirements.
