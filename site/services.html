Accessibility audits and advice

Is your site accessible to people with disabilities? I provide an evaluation of your product or service against recognised international standards such WCAG 2.0/ WCAG 2.1 and Section 508 and can help you with end-to-end support when assessing, implementing and improving your product or service


This accessibility auditing service includes:

  1. Conducting Website and Application reviews/ Assessment and how to make your own products and services accessible
  2. International Accessibility Guidelines WCAG 2.0/ WCAG 2.1, and European/ Irish/ UK standards
  3. Conducting Colour/ design reviews for Accessibility compliance.
  4. Conducting UI/Prototype design reviews for usability compliance.
  5. Work with your team to remediate your website

My Skills/ Credentials are as follows

  • Worked as the Accessibility focal point for an entire product in IBM.
  • Over 7 years experience working in Accessibility in large multinational companies
  • Masters Degree in Assistive technology and dissertation on web accessibility
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.0 and WCAG 2.1), WAI-ARIA, and ADA Section 508 guidelines.
  • Assistive technology such as JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, Dragon and ZoomText.
  • Strong understanding of popular web technologies such as HTML5, JavaScript, CSS3 and WAI-ARIA.
  • Use of accessibility testing tools such as WAT, AXE, WAVE, CCA etc.

The plan is to add new services in the future such as Website A11y training and Assistive Technology asssessment and training
